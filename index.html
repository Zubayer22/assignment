About Titumir College

Government Titumir College is one of the greatest public educational institutions in Bangladesh. In terms of student number it is one of the largest college in the World. It is located on the A.K. Khandakar Road in the posh area of Gulshan, Dhaka. This college is named after Mir Nesar Ali Titumir, the first martyr in the struggle for liberation in the subcontinent.It is affiliated with the University of Dhaka.
